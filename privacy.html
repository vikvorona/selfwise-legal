Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

Selfwise is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your photos and health data stay on your device. We only temporarily process photos for AI analysis, and we never store them on our servers.

Introduction

Selfwise ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Photos

  • Selfie Images: You take daily selfies using the app's camera feature
  • Storage: All photos are stored locally on your device only
  • Processing: Photos are temporarily sent to our AI service for health analysis, then immediately discarded from our servers
  • We do not store your photos on any external servers or cloud storage

Health Data (Apple HealthKit)

  • Data Types: With your permission, we access sleep data, heart rate, and activity data from Apple Health
  • Purpose: This data enhances the personalization of your wellness insights
  • Storage: Health data is never uploaded to external servers
  • Processing: All health data processing occurs locally on your device
  • HealthKit Compliance: We do not use HealthKit data for advertising or share it with third parties

Usage Data

  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data may be collected via Sentry to improve app stability
  • No Personal Data: Crash reports do not contain photos, health data, or personal identifiers

How We Use Your Information

  • AI Health Analysis: Your selfie is sent to Google's Gemini AI to generate wellness insights. The image is processed in real-time and is not stored.
  • Personalized Insights: Health data from Apple Health is used locally to provide context-aware recommendations.
  • App Improvement: Anonymous crash data helps us fix bugs and improve performance.

Data Storage and Security

Local-First Architecture

  • All personal data (photos, health information, preferences) is stored exclusively on your device
  • We use encrypted local storage provided by iOS
  • No cloud backup of your personal data is performed by our app

Data Transmission

  • Photos are transmitted securely via HTTPS for AI analysis
  • Photos are processed in memory and never written to disk on our servers
  • AI analysis requests do not include any personally identifiable information

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI

  • Processes selfie images for health analysis
  • Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • We use Google Gemini's paid API tier, which guarantees that user content is not used to improve Google's products or train their AI models.

RevenueCat

  • Manages subscription purchases
  • Does not receive any health or photo data
  • Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy

Sentry

  • Collects anonymous crash reports
  • Does not receive photos or health data
  • Subject to Sentry's Privacy Policy

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Delete Photos: You can delete any or all photos from within the app
  • Revoke Health Access: You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings
  • Disable Notifications: You can disable notifications in iOS Settings
  • Delete All Data: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

Opt-Out Options

  • Health data access is optional; the app functions without it
  • Push notifications are optional
  • Crash reporting helps improve the app but can be limited by disabling analytics in iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

Selfwise is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Data Retention

  • Photos: Stored until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Health Data: Never stored; only accessed in real-time when needed
  • Preferences: Stored until you uninstall the app
  • Crash Reports: Retained by Sentry for 90 days

International Users

Our servers are located in the European Union (Frankfurt). By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your selfie images to our servers for AI processing.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Apple App Store Requirements

This app complies with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines, including:

  • HealthKit data is used solely for health and fitness purposes
  • HealthKit data is not shared with third parties
  • HealthKit data is not used for advertising
  • Users can revoke HealthKit access at any time
